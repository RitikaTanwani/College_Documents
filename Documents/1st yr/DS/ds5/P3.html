Root a binary tree

Question

Implement a binay tree data structure given the inorder and preorder seqences as the input.
In the input you will be given a value (V) which is present in the tree.
Now write a function to root the tree at the node corresponding to the given value.
You can use your routines from previous assignments (construct trees from pre-order and in-order and also print different traversals).
As output on the first three lines, print the 3 traversal sequences of the resulting binary tree : Inorder, Preorder, Postorder.
Note : Use single rotations (discussed during avl tree re-balancing and splaying) to bring the given node to the root of new tree.

Input Specification

First Line : a number N, which indicates the number of nodes in the trees
Second Line : Pre order sequence
Third Line : In order sequence
Fourth Line : Value (V) which is the new root of the resulting tree.

Output Specification

First Line : Inorder Traversal of the tree
Second Line : Pre-order Traversal of the tree
Third Line : Post-order Traversal of the tree

Sample Input

9
10 5 3 7 6 9 15 11 18
3 5 6 7 9 10 11 15 18
7

Sample Output

3 5 6 7 9 10 11 15 18
7 5 3 6 10 9 15 11 18
3 6 5 9 11 18 15 10 7
